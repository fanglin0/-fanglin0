What can YOU do at home?

You don't have to take to drastic measures. Adapt a entire lifestyle change, cutting plastic out of your life completely would be impossible.

Small changes in your daily routine add up! Being mindful of your plastic use, consider these few tips--

Taking that recyclable bag on your trip to the groceries
Ditch those single-use plastic plates, cups, and cutleries! They are definitely usable in large-scale dining parties, but in daily live sonider opting for quality silverware instead.
Buy in bulk! This one isn't as obvious, but buying bulk is 1) cheaper, and 2) uses less plastic in packaging.
Lastly, use a reusable bottle! Microplastic can leech from plastic bottles into your drinking water, and conitnually using plastic bottles can get expensive! Reusable bottle is better and healthier in all ways.
